Our Mission

To create a transparent, automated, and profitable ecosystem for renewable energy producers and carbon offset buyers using IoT + Blockchain + Web3 technologies.

IoT Device Setup

Follow these steps to connect your ESP32 device to your solar panels

Step 1: Mount the Device

Securely mount the ESP32 device near your solar panel array. Ensure it has access to power (USB or solar) and Wi-Fi signal.

Step 2: Power On

Connect the device to power via USB cable or solar panel output. The LED should blink blue indicating it's in setup mode.

Step 3: Connect to Wi-Fi

On your phone, connect to the Wi-Fi network named "SolaNX-Setup". This is a temporary network created by your device.

Step 4: Pair with Account

Scan the QR code on your device or enter the device ID manually in your dashboard to complete pairing.

Technical Specifications

Device: ESP32 with MQTT protocol | Frequency: Reports every 5 minutes | Power: 5V USB or 12V solar | Range: 50m Wi-Fi | Compatibility: All major solar panel brands
